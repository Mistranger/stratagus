FreeCraft tileset graphic format

The freecraft playfield is build with tiles each with the size of 32x32 pixels.

Tileset graphic file format

The graphic data for tiles are stored in the directory: $FREECRAFTHOME/data/graphic/tileset.

Tileset map file format

The tiles are stored in the map with this numbers.

The mapping from the map tile number to the graphic tile number is stored in the file $FREECRAFTHOME/data/ccl/tileset.ccl.

The tiles in the map are numbered from 0 to 0x9DF (2527).

  • 0x0000 - 0x000F unused
  • 0x0010 - 0x001F solid light water
  • 0x0020 - 0x002F solid dark water
  • 0x0030 - 0x003F solid light coast
  • 0x0040 - 0x004F solid dark coast
  • 0x0050 - 0x005F solid light ground
  • 0x0060 - 0x006F solid dark ground
  • 0x0070 - 0x007F solid forest
  • 0x0080 - 0x008F solid mountains
  • 0x0090 - 0x009F solid human walls
    • 0x0 Solid wall
    • 0x2 defect wall
    • 0x4 destroyed wall
  • 0x00A0 - 0x00AF solid orc walls
    See solid human walls.
  • 0x00B0 - 0x00BF solid human walls
    See solid human walls.
  • 0x00C0 - 0x00CF solid orc walls
    See solid human walls.
  • 0x00D0 - 0x00FF unused
  • 0x0100 - 0x01FF dark and light water
    • 0x0* filled upper left
    • 0x1* filled upper right
    • 0x2* filled upper half
    • 0x3* filled lower left
    • 0x4* filled left half
    • 0x5* clear upper left and lower right
    • 0x6* clear lower right
    • 0x7* filled lower right
    • 0x8* filled upper left and lower right
    • 0x9* clear left half
    • 0xA* clear lower left
    • 0xB* clear upper half
    • 0xC* clear upper right
    • 0xD* clear upper left
    • 0xE* unused
    • 0xF* unused
  • 0x0200 - 0x02FF water and coast
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0300 - 0x03FF dark and light coast
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0400 - 0x04FF mountain and coast
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0500 - 0x05FF coast and ground
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0600 - 0x06FF dark and light ground
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0700 - 0x07FF forest and ground
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0800 - 0x08FF human wall
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0900 - 0x09FF orc wall
    See dark and light water
  • 0x0A00 - 0xFFFF unused
